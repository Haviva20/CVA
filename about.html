Foundation

CVA was founded in December 2016, as (Who we are) a not-for-profit women's sports organization enthusiastic
about developing female athletes' potential while championing charity and supporting girl child education.

We are motivated by the desire to use sports to inspire female youths in communities to live healthier lives, through
mentorship and life skills training.

Foundation based on;

  • Enthusiasm

    • We use our passion for volleyball to bring Women together to reach and inspire women in the community in
    a fun way.

  • Charity

    • We use our founded passion for Volleyball to encourage youths to stay in school and pursue and complete
    their academic studies by extending education help to school-going players and memebers.

  • Education

    • We use volleyball to extend unconditional assistance to disadvantaged groups within the communities that
    host CVA and its activities or programs.

Leadership

    Our leadership structure has three bodies whose roles are consultative.

  • The Congress

    • This comes in place every time the members convene at the Annual General Assembly. It is the policy-
    making body of the Club.

  • The Board of Administration

    • This is composed of the Founder Members (03), the Club President, the Technical Director, the Club
    treasurer, and one member elected by the congress to represent it on the Board.

  • The Management Committee

    • The committee is charged with the day-to-day running of affairs of the Club. It is composed of elective posts
    which include; President, General Secretary, Treasurer, manager, Coordinator, and Captain.

Athlete's Opportunities

Youths' athletes participating in sports under CVA get the opportunity to;

  • Belong to an organized Female youth group.
  • Engage in charity and humanitarian activities.
  • Engage in community sports festivals and fundraising drives or
  • Have improved personal character, self-esteem, and condidence
  • be positive role models to fellow youths
  • Be exposed to compititive sports, and later elite volleyball and professional competitions.
  • Employment opportunities on sports-related career opportunities.
